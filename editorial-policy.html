Editorial Policy

How we maintain independence and integrity in our reviews

Our Commitment to Independence

At Curated Picks, editorial independence is the foundation of everything we do. We believe our readers deserve honest, unbiased recommendations that prioritize their needs over commercial relationships.

Our editorial team operates separately from our business operations. Product recommendations are never influenced by affiliate relationships, advertising partnerships, or brand preferences.

How We Select Products

Every product featured on Curated Picks goes through a rigorous selection process:

  • Market Research: We analyze hundreds of products in each category, examining specifications, features, and value propositions.
  • Expert Consultation: We consult with industry experts, professional reviewers, and experienced users to understand real-world performance.
  • Hands-On Testing: Our team tests products in real-world conditions for a minimum of two weeks before making recommendations.
  • Performance Metrics: We measure objective performance data whenever possible, using standardized testing methodologies.

Our Testing Process

We believe thorough testing is the only way to provide genuine value to our readers. Here's how we test:

  • Real-World Usage: Products are used in everyday scenarios, not just controlled laboratory conditions.
  • Long-Term Evaluation: We assess durability and performance over time, not just initial impressions.
  • Comparison Testing: We test products side-by-side with competitors to identify genuine advantages and disadvantages.
  • Multiple Use Cases: We evaluate products across different scenarios to understand their versatility.

Affiliate Relationships

Curated Picks participates in affiliate programs, including the Amazon Associates program. This means we may earn commissions when you purchase products through our links.

Important: These relationships do not influence our editorial decisions. We recommend products based on merit, not commission rates. Our recommendations would be the same whether or not we earn affiliate commissions.

We only link to products we genuinely believe will provide value to our readers. If a product doesn't meet our standards, we won't recommend it, regardless of potential affiliate earnings.

Corrections and Updates

We're committed to accuracy. If you believe we've made an error in our reviews or recommendations, please contact us. We regularly update our content to reflect:

  • New product releases and discontinuations
  • Price changes and availability updates
  • Performance issues discovered after publication
  • Improvements to our testing methodologies

Reader Trust

Our readers' trust is our most valuable asset. We maintain that trust by:

  • Being transparent about our affiliate relationships
  • Disclosing any potential conflicts of interest
  • Publishing both positive and negative findings
  • Responding to reader feedback and concerns
  • Never accepting payment for positive reviews
