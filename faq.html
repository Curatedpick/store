Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Curated Picks

About Us

What is Curated Picks?

Curated Picks is an independent product review and recommendation website. We test hundreds of products across multiple categories and provide honest, unbiased recommendations to help you make informed purchasing decisions.

How do you make money?

We earn commissions from retailers like Amazon when you purchase products through our affiliate links. This doesn't affect our recommendations - we only suggest products we genuinely believe in, regardless of commission rates.

Do you accept free products from brands?

We occasionally accept product loans for review purposes, but these are always returned after testing. We never accept free products in exchange for positive reviews. All our recommendations are independent and based on merit.

Our Review Process

How do you test products?

Every product is tested in real-world conditions for at least two weeks. We use them daily, just like you would. We measure performance metrics, compare with competitors, and evaluate durability over time. Our testing process is thorough and unbiased.

How often do you update your recommendations?

We review and update our recommendations monthly, or more frequently if new products are released or prices change significantly. Our deals section is updated hourly to ensure you're always seeing the best current offers.

Can I suggest a product for review?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our readers. Use our contact form to suggest products you'd like us to test. We prioritize suggestions that multiple readers request.

Shopping & Purchases

Do you sell products directly?

No, we don't sell products directly. We provide recommendations and link to retailers like Amazon where you can make purchases. When you click our links and buy something, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

Are prices on your site accurate?

We strive to keep prices updated, but prices on retailer sites can change frequently. Always check the current price on the retailer's website before making a purchase. We update our deals section hourly to minimize discrepancies.

Do you ship internationally?

We don't ship products ourselves. However, many of the retailers we link to (like Amazon) ship internationally. Product availability and prices may vary by location. We primarily focus on products available in the US market but are expanding to other regions.

Trust & Transparency

How do I know your reviews are unbiased?

We follow strict editorial guidelines. Our writers and editors don't know which products will perform best in testing. We buy most products ourselves and return loans after review. Our affiliate relationships don't influence rankings. Read our Editorial Policy for more details.

What if I disagree with a recommendation?

We welcome respectful disagreement! Different people have different needs and preferences. If you have feedback about a specific review, please contact us. We appreciate hearing diverse perspectives and may update our reviews based on valid feedback.

Do you remove negative reviews?

No. We publish both positive and negative findings. If a product doesn't meet our standards, we won't recommend it, even if it has good affiliate potential. Transparency and honesty are core to our mission.

Technical & Account

Do I need to create an account?

No, you don't need an account to browse our recommendations. However, creating a free account allows you to save products to your wishlist, track recently viewed items, and receive our weekly newsletter.

How do I unsubscribe from the newsletter?

Every newsletter email includes an unsubscribe link at the bottom. Click it to instantly unsubscribe. You can also contact us if you need help.

Is the site mobile-friendly?

Yes! Our website is fully responsive and works great on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile. You can browse and shop from anywhere.

Still Have Questions?

If you didn't find the answer you were looking for, please don't hesitate to reach out. We're here to help!
