Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency about our affiliate relationships and how we earn

Our Affiliate Relationships

Curated Picks is a participant in various affiliate advertising programs designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to retail partners.

This means that when you click on certain links on our website and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you.

Amazon Associates Program

Curated Picks is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com and affiliated sites.

As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases. This means that when you click on an Amazon link on our website and buy something, we receive a small percentage of the sale.

Amazon, the Amazon logo, AmazonSupply, and the AmazonSupply logo are trademarks of Amazon.com, Inc. or its affiliates.

Other Affiliate Programs

In addition to Amazon, we may participate in affiliate programs with other retailers and brands. These may include but are not limited to:

  • Electronics retailers
  • Home goods stores
  • Fashion and apparel brands
  • Software and service providers
  • Other e-commerce platforms

The specific affiliate programs we participate in may change over time as we evaluate new opportunities to serve our readers better.

How This Affects You

No Additional Cost

When you purchase products through our affiliate links, you pay the same price as you would if you went directly to the retailer's website. The commission we earn comes from the retailer, not from you.

No Influence on Recommendations

Our affiliate relationships do not influence our product recommendations or reviews. We recommend products based on their merit, quality, and value to our readers, not based on the commission rates we receive.

We would recommend the same products whether or not we earn affiliate commissions. Our editorial independence is paramount, and we maintain strict separation between our business operations and our editorial decisions.

Honest Reviews

We provide honest reviews of all products we feature, including both positive and negative aspects. If a product doesn't meet our standards, we won't recommend it, even if it has high commission potential.

Why We Use Affiliate Links

Affiliate commissions help us:

  • Maintain our website: Hosting, development, and maintenance costs are covered by affiliate revenue.
  • Fund product testing: We purchase many products ourselves to test them thoroughly.
  • Pay our team: Our writers, editors, and researchers are compensated for their work.
  • Keep content free: Affiliate revenue allows us to provide all our content free to readers.
  • Remain independent: Affiliate income helps us stay independent and avoid sponsored content or paid reviews.

Without affiliate commissions, we would need to rely on advertising, sponsored content, or paid subscriptions, which could compromise our editorial independence.

Our Commitment to Transparency

We believe in full transparency with our readers. Here's what we commit to:

  • Clear disclosure: We disclose our affiliate relationships on this page and in our website footer.
  • Honest recommendations: We only recommend products we genuinely believe in.
  • No paid reviews: We never accept payment from brands in exchange for positive reviews.
  • Editorial independence: Our editorial team operates independently from our business operations.
  • Quality first: We prioritize quality and value for our readers above commission rates.

How to Identify Affiliate Links

Affiliate links on our website may look like regular links, but they contain tracking parameters that allow us to earn commissions. Here's how you can identify them:

  • Links to Amazon typically include our associate tag in the URL
  • Some affiliate links may redirect through our domain before reaching the retailer
  • We may use link shortening services for affiliate links

While we strive to make affiliate links identifiable, the most reliable indicator is our editorial context. If we're recommending a product and providing a link to purchase it, it's likely an affiliate link.

FTC Compliance

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidelines on endorsements and testimonials, we disclose our material connections to the products and services we recommend.

The FTC requires that we disclose any "material connection" we have with the brands whose products we review or recommend. A material connection is a relationship that might affect the weight or credibility that consumers give to an endorsement.

Our affiliate relationships constitute material connections, and we disclose them as required by the FTC.

When you use our website and make purchases through our links:

  • You pay the same price as you would going directly to the retailer

  • You benefit from our thorough research and testing

We appreciate your support and trust. Our goal is to help you make informed purchasing decisions while maintaining the highest standards of editorial integrity.
